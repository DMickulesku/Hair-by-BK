As a seasoned stylist,

Brook loves all aspects of hair, especially color. In 2016, Brook began taking multiple Advanced Color Courses at the Wella Academy in New York pursuing a Master Colorist Certificate and most recently was also certified at Calligraphy Cutting. She has also participated in multiple advanced education classes, including Vidal Sassoon Cutting, Wella color, Bumble and Bumble cutting and Oribe and Kerastase styling classes. Brook describes herself as kind of “quirky” but very laid back and easy to talk to. Even though it doesn’t take much to inspire a stylist, Brook spends a lot of time following fashion blogs, magazines and pop culture. Her co-workers, friends and clients also inspire her. When not working, Brook loves trying new restaurants, cooking, reading, music and fashion. If you are looking for a great new stylist who is sure to leave you feeling beautiful, Brook is your stylist!
